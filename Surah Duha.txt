Verse 1: By the morning sunlight,
Verse 2: and the night when it falls still!
Verse 3: Your Lord O Prophet has not abandoned you, nor has He become hateful of you.
Verse 4: and the next life is certainly far better for you than this one.
Verse 5: And surely your Lord will give so much to you that you will be pleased.
Verse 6: Did He not find you as an orphan then sheltered you?
Verse 7: Did He not find you unguided then guided you?
Verse 8: And did He not find you needy then satisfied your needs?
Verse 9: So do not oppress the orphan,
Verse 10: nor repulse the beggar.
Verse 11: And proclaim the blessings of your Lord.
