Verse 1: By the sun and its brightness,
Verse 2: and the moon as it follows it,
Verse 3: and the day as it unveils it,
Verse 4: and the night as it conceals it!
Verse 5: And by heaven and the One Who built it,
Verse 6: and the earth and the One Who spread it!
Verse 7: And by the soul and the One Who fashioned it,
Verse 8: then with the knowledge of right and wrong inspired it!
Verse 9: Successful indeed is the one who purifies their soul,
Verse 10: and doomed is the one who corrupts it!
Verse 11: Thamud rejected the truth out of arrogance,
Verse 12: when the most wicked of them was roused to kill the she-camel.
Verse 13: But the messenger of Allah warned them, "Do not disturb Allah's camel and her turn to drink!"
Verse 14: Still they defied him and slaughtered her. So their Lord crushed them for their crime, levelling all to the ground.
Verse 15: He has no fear of consequences.
